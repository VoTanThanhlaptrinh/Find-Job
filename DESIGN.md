# Design System Strategy: Modern Precision

## Visual Identity
This design system is built on a foundation of clarity, professionalism, and modern technical utility. The aesthetic prioritizes functional elegance, using a vibrant blue-centric palette to convey trust and innovation.

## Color Palette
The color strategy employs a "fidelity" approach, ensuring high-contrast and intentional application of brand colors.
- **Primary**: #2563EB (Electric Blue) – Used for primary actions and brand presence.
- **Secondary**: #0EA5E9 (Sky Blue) – Supporting interactive elements and highlights.
- **Tertiary**: #A855F7 (Amethyst) – Accents and badge elements for visual interest.
- **Neutral**: #F8FAFC (Cool Slate) – A clean, modern foundation for surfaces and backgrounds.

## Typography
The system uses a dual-font strategy to balance character with readability.
- **Headlines**: Manrope – A geometric sans-serif that brings a modern, tech-forward personality to page hierarchy.
- **Body & Labels**: Inter – A highly legible typeface designed for user interfaces, ensuring clarity across all screen sizes and densities.

## UI Geometry & Space
The interface transitions from an editorial feel to a more streamlined, balanced layout.
- **Roundedness (Level 2)**: Moderate corner radii provide a soft, approachable feel without appearing overly playful, maintaining a professional demeanor.
- **Spacing (Level 2)**: The layout follows a "Normal" density profile. This ensures a balanced distribution of whitespace that is efficient for data-heavy applications while remaining clear and uncluttered for standard content consumption. This shift from spacious to normal allows for higher information density while maintaining visual breathing room.
