# Recruiter Portal Site Structure

## Vision
A premium tracking and management suite for hiring managers, focused on clarity, speed, and candidate tracking workflows.

## Roadmap (Screens to Generate)
- [ ] Dashboard Shell Component (Sidebar + Top bar navigation)
- [ ] Dashboard Overview (Metrics, recent activity, funnel charts)
- [ ] Job List (Grid or heavy-table of job postings managed by the recruiter)
- [ ] Post Job (Form interface split into Basic Info and Rich Text Requirements)
- [ ] Job Detail (Read-only view of a specific job post)
- [ ] Candidate Tracking List (Funnel/List view of candidates applying for specific jobs)
- [ ] Company Address (Management interface for locations)
